# Paper Reading

Use this skill when the user asks to read, summarize, compare, or critique an
academic paper.

## Workflow

1. Identify the paper title, authors, venue, year, and main research question.
2. Extract the method into a short pipeline: inputs, core idea, training or
   inference process, and outputs.
3. Summarize experiments by dataset, baseline, metric, and conclusion.
4. Separate confirmed claims from your own interpretation.
5. End with limitations, useful follow-up experiments, and open questions.

## Output

Answer in Chinese unless the user asks otherwise. Prefer compact sections:

- 核心问题
- 方法概览
- 实验结论
- 局限与疑问
- 对当前项目的启发
